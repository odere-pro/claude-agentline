- Implement doctor check D07 — pricing freshness. The check parses `PRICING_TABLE_VERSION` from `src/tokens/pricing.ts`, reports `pass` while the embedded pricing table is within 90 days of `now`, `warn` once it crosses the threshold (with the version date and age in days surfaced in the message), and `warn` on a malformed or calendar-invalid version. Future-dated versions are treated as fresh and reported as `0d old`. The check remains report-only — no `--fix` action — per spec §9.2.
