- Remove the `time` and `custom` widget families: drop the `clock`, `uptime-session`, `uptime-block` (time) and `separator`, `osc-link` (custom) widgets, leaving a five-family catalogue (`session`, `tokens`, `context`, `rate-limits`, `git`). The `formatClock` wall-clock token formatter the surviving reset-at widgets share moves into the rate-limits family; the global `separator` join string and the deterministic render-clock (`--frozen-clock`, `ctx.clock`) are unaffected. The editor's osc-link url/label options prompt is retired with the widget. **Breaking:** configs naming `clock`, `uptime-session`, `uptime-block`, `separator`, or `osc-link` render nothing and emit an `agentline: unknown widget type '…'` warning line; an unregistered widget type now carries no family glyph or accent instead of falling back to the removed `custom` family. The shipped catalogue drops from 34 to 29 widgets (7 → 5 families).
